Tech I enjoy:

Software - Omarchy

Of all OS I have tested, Omarchy has been the most impressive. Built up from Arch linux, the ricing makes it accessible even for new linux users (like me!).
Omarchy makes for customizable a flexible and stable OS.


Software - Samsung DEX

Samsung Galaxy S devices have the ability to simulate a desktop environment with Android.
Combined with an app like RustDesk (open-source), you can remote access a Windows or Linux PC and use it from your phone or tablet, thus essencialy having a full x86 architecture on the go with easy access to android apps without the trouble of emulation.


Hardware - Womier WK61

A decent budget mechanical keyboard in the 60% format that is very reliable. North facing LEDs with unfortunate no VIA support, cherry red linear switches (pre-lubed btw).
