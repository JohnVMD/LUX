Software - Omarchy

Of all OS I have tested, Omarchy has been the most impressive. Built up from Arch linux, the ricing makes it accessible even for new linux users (like me!).
Omarchy takes the ease of use from win10 and the endless possibilities that Arch offers, combining a bunch of great tools into a flexible and stable OS.
